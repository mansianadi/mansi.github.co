Description

I designed a book cover for a client who was the author as well. The book talks about the economy of the top seven countries which are ranked according to the GDP (Gross Domestic Product) during the pandemic. It also touches upon the history, growth and how they developed while also describing the economic model that might work for different countries. I designed the book cover where I used seven squares in different colors and connected them in various ways to show that the economies of the countries are dependent on one another.
